Animal Science Exploration Day

Discover the World of Animal Science
May 3, 2017

What is it?

Animal Science Exploration Day is a FREE, one-day event held at the University of Missouri Animal Science Research Center. Participants will have the opportunity to meet current students and faculty, learn about careers in Animal Science, participate in hands-on activities, and tour university farms.

Who is it for?

This event is for high school sophomores and juniors interested in a future in Animal Science. By participating in ASED students will gain a better understanding of the current Animal Science curriculum as well as opportunities available during, and after, their college career.
