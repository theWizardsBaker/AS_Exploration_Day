Animal Science Exploration Day

What is it?

Animal Science Exploration Day is a one day event put on by the Division of Animal Sciences at the University of Missouri. This event includes hands-on activites, tours of the University Farms and Vet School, and a chance to meet both current and alumni students.

Who is it for?

Sophomores and Juniors in High School who are intereted in learning about Animal Sciences.
